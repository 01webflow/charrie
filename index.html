Vocational Skills Training:

WASAF’s vocational training equips young people with practical skills, such as agriculture, carpentry, and technology, preparing them for employment and economic independence. This program empowers youth with the tools they need to support themselves and their families.

Pure Drinking Water

Access to pure drinking water is a critical focus for Wawa Seed Africa Foundation (WASAF). In many of the communities we serve, clean water is scarce, impacting health, sanitation, and overall quality of life.
